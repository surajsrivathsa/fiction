TO THE PUBLIC: - These books are sold by news dealers everywhere. If your dealer does not keep them, and will not get them for you, send direct to the publishers, in which case four cents must be added to the price per copy to cover postage. In order that there may be no confusion, we desire to say that the books listed above will be issued, during the respective months, in New York City and vicinity.
