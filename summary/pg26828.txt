Esther lives with her widower father who lives sorrowing after his deceased wife. He pays very little attention to his daughter except to to have her run the household. Esther finds a friend in Pitt who she loves for his brotherly attention and for all he teaches her about history, nature and geography. Her simple uncomplicated life is filled with loneliness and Pitt is a great teacher. Esther finds her mother's Bible and seeks solace and comfort to face her challenges.
