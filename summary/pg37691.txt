Scene - An Excursion Agent's Offices. Behind the counters polite and patient Clerks are besieged by a crowd of Intending Tourists, all asking questions at once. First Intending Tourist. Here - have you made out that estimate for me yet? Clerk. In one moment, Sir. He refers to a list, turns over innumerable books, jots down columns of francs, marks, and florins; reduces them to English money, and adds them up. First class fares on the Rhine, Danube and Black Sea steamers, I think you said, second class rail, and postwagen?
